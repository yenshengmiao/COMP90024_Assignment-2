This scenario tries to explore whether people who visit green spaces regularly will be happier or not. By compare positive rate of tweets and proportion that people weekly visit green space in each suburb. From the graph we can observe that the fluctuations of both lines tend to be consistent. Therefore, aside from other factors, people who visit green place like park or garden frequently are more likely to be positive.
